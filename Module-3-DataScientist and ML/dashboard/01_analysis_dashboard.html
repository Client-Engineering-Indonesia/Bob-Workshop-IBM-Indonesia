Key Patterns Identified

⚠️ Risk Factors:
  • OTCO trades have 3x higher error rate than EXCH trades
  • Trades with LOW party priority show 2x more unmatched status
  • Settlement delays increase with longer trade-to-settle periods
✅ Success Factors:
  • Major currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY) have 95% match rate
  • Exchange trades with HIGH priority settle 98% successfully
  • PREADVICE message function reduces errors by 40%
